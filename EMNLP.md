## Key Trends

1. **2019**: Early NLP work on financial events, forex/stock prediction, complaint analysis
2. **2020-2022**: Quiet period, mainly benchmarks and evaluations
3. **2023**: Surge in financial LLM work with FinBERT variants and domain-specific models
4. **2024**: Focus on parameter-efficient tuning, smaller models for financial QA
5. **2025**: LLM agents in finance, multilingual benchmarks, comprehensive surveys

---

## Notes

- **ECONLP Workshop**: 2019 was particularly active for financial domain NLP
- **LLM Revolution**: Since 2023, major focus on evaluating and improving LLMs for finance
- **Benchmarks**: Multiple benchmarks created to evaluate financial models (FinBERT, FiLM, etc.)
- **QA Focus**: Growing interest in financial question answering over documents
- **Multilingual**: 2025 includes bilingual and multilingual benchmarks
