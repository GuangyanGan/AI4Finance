## Workshops

### FinIR - Financial Information Retrieval

- **FinIR 2020**: 1st Workshop on Financial Information Retrieval (co-located with SIGIR 2020)
  - Focus: IR challenges in finance, financial document retrieval
  - Grand challenge: Commodity market prediction with financial news and analyst reports
  - Multi-language financial document retrieval

- **FinIR 2025**: 2nd Workshop on Financial Information Retrieval in the Era of Generative AI
  - Co-located with SIGIR 2025 (July 17, 2025, Padua, Italy)
  - Organizers: Bloomberg AI Research team (Shuo Zhang)
  - Focus: Generative AI + IR for finance
  - Topics: Financial retrieval models, table retrieval, multi-modal retrieval, real-time retrieval, generative retrieval

### Research Topics at FinIR

- **Retrieval Models**: Table retrieval, multi-modal retrieval, real-time retrieval, generative retrieval
- **Data Processing**: Financial data preprocessing and normalization
- **Applications**: Stock price prediction, stock recommendation, portfolio optimization
- **Content Generation**: Financial content generation using generative models
- **Event Forecasting**: Financial event prediction and early warning systems
